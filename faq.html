Frequently Asked Questions

What is Fundsaudit?

Fundsaudit is a simple digital financial auditing app that automatically fetches and categorizes your income and expenses to help you understand your money flow, savings capacity, spending habits, and financial health. It is developed by Fundsroom, a Startup India-registered and ISO-certified fintech company founded in 2020.

What exactly does the app audit?

Fundsaudit audits: Expenses (rent, EMI, bills, groceries, travel, etc.), Spending patterns, Savings vs spending ratio, Budget deviations, Financial behaviour scores. All are generated automatically using AI-based classification.

Does Fundsaudit provide investment or stock market data?

No. Fundsaudit is only for income and expense auditing-not for stock analysis or mutual fund tracking.

Is Fundsaudit safe?

Yes. Fundsaudit uses: ISO 27001:2013 certified information security practices, Bank-level AES-256 encryption, Zero Data Sharing Policy, Read-only access to statements, No "edit" or "transaction" permissions. Your financial transactions remain untouched.

How does Fundsaudit fetch my income and expenses automatically?

The app uses AI-enabled smart statement reading. It fetches through message API.

Does the app access my bank account directly?

No. It only uses: User-uploaded statements OR Read-only secure fetch (where enabled). It cannot make transfers, withdrawals, or payments.

Can someone steal my money through this app?

No. Fundsaudit has zero transactional capability. It analyzes statements, but cannot initiate any transaction.

Will my personal data be sold?

No. Fundsaudit follows a strict No Third-Party Data Sharing policy and handles data according to global ISO standards.

What does the Fundsaudit report include?

The audit report provides: Total monthly, weekly, as well as daily incomes. Total monthly, weekly, as well as daily expenses. Spending categories. Essential vs non-essential breakdown. Saving percentage. Cash flow surplus/deficit. Debt-tracking & EMI overview. Alerts for overspending. Personalized budgeting suggestions.

Does the app tell me how to save money?

Yes. It provides: Smart saving tips, Overspending alerts, Expense-reduction suggestions, Ideal budget distribution (50:30:20, 60:20:20, etc.), Monthly financial score.

How often is my audit updated?

Every time a statement is uploaded. Automatically if a user enables monthly sync.

Can Fundsaudit handle multiple bank accounts?

Yes. Users can upload statements from multiple banks and the app consolidates them into a single financial view.

Is the app free to use?

NO, the subscription is for Rs 99/- annually. Basic auditing is free. Advanced financial insights may come under premium plans later.

What is required during onboarding?

Users need to provide: Name, Email, Phone number, OTP verification.

Does onboarding take long?

No. Registration takes 1 minute, and full audit generation takes 2-3 minutes.

What if someone doesn't want to upload a bank statement?

They can still explore the app features. Uploading a statement simply enables detailed audit insights.

Why should I use Fundsaudit?

Because most people in India: Don't track expenses daily, Overspend unknowingly, Don't know where their salary goes, Have no monthly budgeting, Don't follow saving discipline. Fundsaudit solves this by giving a clear, automated monthly audit.

How accurate is the expense categorization?

Fundsaudit's AI model is trained on lakhs of financial entries, giving an accuracy level of 90%+ in correctly categorizing expenses.

What makes Fundsaudit different from budgeting apps?

Unlike manual budgeting apps, Fundsaudit is: Automatic (no manual entry), AI-powered, Secure & ISO-certified, Focused on financial auditing, not just expense tracking. It builds a complete financial health picture.

Will using Fundsaudit improve my financial habits?

Yes. Users who track expenses regularly improve savings by 25-40% on average (based on global financial behaviour studies).

I didn't receive OTP. What should I do?

Check network, Ensure email/phone is correct, Try "Resend OTP", Check spam folder.

My statement didn't upload. Why?

Possible reasons: there is no need to upload any bank details, statement etc.

My expenses aren't categorized correctly.

You can manually adjust categories. The AI improves based on user corrections.

Does the app support all banks?

All banks, major Indian banks (SBI, HDFC, ICICI, Axis, Kotak, etc.) AND several smaller banks are supported.

Who can see my financial details?

Only you. i.e the owner -customers individual only. Interns only see "onboarding status," not your personal financial numbers.

Can I delete my audit or data?

Yes. Users can request data deletion anytime.

If I uninstall the app, what happens to my data?

You can choose to delete data permanently. Or leave it stored securely (read-only) for future audits.

Is this a loan app?

No. Fundsaudit does not provide loans, credit cards, or any banking service.

Will this affect my CIBIL score?

No. The app only analyzes income and expenses-it has no impact on credit score.

Does the app ask for bank login details?

Never. It only uses statements or read-only authorization.

Can Fundsaudit transfer money without my permission?

No. It does not have access to bank login, UPI, or account numbers for transactions.

Will I get spam messages after onboarding?

No. Fundsaudit does not share data with any third party.

Why are interns onboarding users?

To help: Spread financial awareness, Teach people how to audit their monthly finances, Help families understand money flow, Collect real-world behaviour insights.

Is it mandatory to upload statements to complete onboarding?

No, it is not recommended.

What do I get after onboarding?

You receive: Full income & expense audit, Financial health rating, Budget suggestions, Spending habit insights, Monthly report.

How do I know this app is legitimate?

Because: Built by Fundsroom, Startup India-recognized, Company founded in 2020, ISO-certified for data security, Uses bank-standard encryption, Has transparent read-only access model.

Should I trust the audit report?

Yes. The analysis is generated using accurate classification algorithms trained on lakhs of transaction records.
